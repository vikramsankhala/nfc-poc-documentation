## üìù Investor Pitch Customization

### For Fintech VCs:
- Emphasize: Market size, regulatory compliance, banking relationships
- Highlight: Customer traction, revenue model, unit economics
- Show: Banking partnerships, pilot programs, case studies

### For Strategic Investors:
- Emphasize: Partnership opportunities, customer access, integration potential
- Highlight: Technology differentiation, competitive advantages
- Show: Product roadmap, integration capabilities, scalability

### For AI/ML VCs:
- Emphasize: AI technology, ML models, competitive moat
- Highlight: Data advantages, algorithm performance, AI team
- Show: Technical architecture, AI capabilities, innovation

### For Enterprise SaaS VCs:
- Emphasize: SaaS metrics, recurring revenue, customer retention
- Highlight: Sales efficiency, CAC payback, LTV/CAC ratio
- Show: Growth trajectory, expansion revenue, churn rates
